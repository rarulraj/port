All About Me

Hello! I'm Arun, a Game Developer and Programmer based in California. I have been passionately creating games since 2018, showcasing my skills across a variety of different game engines. In addition to my proficiency in programming, I also possess expertise in pixel art using tools such as Aseprite and GIMP, as well as 3D art using Blender. Currently, I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in both Computer Science and Chemistry from Cal State University East Bay, with an expected graduation date in 2023. This portfolio showcases a collection of my personal projects and fun creations, highlighting my specialization in Game Development. If you have any inquiries or would like to learn more, please feel free to refer to my resume and find my contact information below.

Powerless
Choice Based RPG(Unity 2D)

This was my first foray into the RPG genre. I had done some unity work previously but this game includes it's own dialogue system and weather cycle. Demonstrates proficency with C# and game systems. All 400 of the unique sprites and pixel art tiles are made by me as well using Asesprite and GIMP.

UDrone
Wave Based Shooter(Unreal Engine)

A wave based shooter using Unreal's Blueprinting System. Ai and Particle effects as well were made by me. Shows understanding of Blueprinting and Physics systems in Unreal. Also demonstrates understanding of Unreal's UI system.

Rainbow Runner
Endless Runner(Unreal Engine)

An endless runner game with power ups sprinkled throughout the levels. The map generates different obstacles as you run. Made using C++ in combination with Unreal's blueprinting system. Demonstrates proficency in C++ and an understanding of level design.

Voxel Shooter
A Multiplayer Shooter(Unreal Engine)

An online based Multiplayer shooter. Uses voxel based enviroments as maps. There are multiple classes to choose from at start. Multiple game modes such as Team deathmatch and capture the flag. Demonstrates understanding of servers and C++.

Magnet Puzzles
A VR puzzle game(Unreal)

Uses Unreal's VR capabilites to build a gun that can magnetize world objects.Using the objects to move puzzles and areas in the level. Demonstrates understanding of VR and Animation in Unreal using C++ and Blueprinting. Demonstrates knowledge of puzzle level design and game principles.
